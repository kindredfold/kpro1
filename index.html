Yo! I'm Christian.

I started Kindred Fold originally with a friend as a way to bring our photographic and film collaborations together under one unified brand. This was birthed out of a time where I was doing a residency in multidisciplinary documentary storytelling; creating podcasts, capturing stories and events through photo and video, and producing large-scale live productions in Austin, TX.

I now live in Portland, OR, but continue to collaborate with many different creatives and agencies on projects as well as freelance independently. I find great joy in working on community focused projects that celebrate voices and lives rarely heard from in our own personal spheres. I am currently active in editorial and commercial advertising media and production, but offer a vast array of creative services from project production to capture, deployment, and display.

My passion is creating amazing images, stories, and experiences. I love partnering with clients and brands that desire to show the world, through shared media and curated experiences, more about the deep and often hidden meaningfulness in everything and everyone.

If you are a company, a brand, or simply a person with a story to share and want to see it crafted into a concise and eloquent image or story, let's talk. We would love to hear from you.
